Welcome to the website for Orange County Basketball Officials Association (OCBOA)

Located in Section 9, Orange County, New York. OCBOA is a member of the International Association of Approved Basketball Officials (IAABO), Board #180 in addition we are a member of the NYS Girls Basketball Association. OCBOA is dedicated to providing the highest levels of professionalism and quality officiating to basketball programs throughout our service area.

The purpose of the Orange County Basketball Officials

Association is to further the interests of amateur basketball officials; promote the welfare of amateur basketball, its players, officials, administrators, fans and the media; develop, promote and maintain the highest standards of sportsmanship in connection with the game of basketball; provide educational programs to advance the skills of amateurbasketball officials; work with sports administrators to provide qualified individuals to officiate basketball games; and encourage uniform interpretation of the rules of basketball.
